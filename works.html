[ Encounters — wide production photo ]
2017 – 18 Actor / Writer

Encounters

OurTheatre, Bangalore · University of South Florida, USA

An hour-long work-in-progress theatre piece about a man navigating an ever-growing maze, encountering figures from his own memory. Derived from a series of poems, the piece layers actions, sounds, images, rhythms, songs and text to depict temporary landscapes of space and time. Opened at OurTheatre Bangalore in October 2017; performed at USF in February 2018.

Sapna

A Moment of Memory

Visual Respiration, Bangalore · Chennai

A devised play built on personal memories and songs taught by the artist's mother. Created through expressive gestures, movement, topography, tempo, duration and repetition. Developed with Visual Respiration Theatre Company.
