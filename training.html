Methodology

Devising Tools

Text

Literary source texts, poems, and prose as dramaturgical anchors and starting points.

Movement Cells

Repeated physical scores built through exploration and partner work in the studio.

Montage & Haiku

Juxtaposition of images and micro-narratives across time and space.

Partner Work

Relational scores built through listening, impulse and response between performers.

Object Theatre

Objects as characters and active dramaturgical partners in the space.

Sound & Rhythm

Songs, rhythmic patterns and vocal textures as structural and atmospheric elements.

Personal Narrative

Autobiographical memory as raw material for devising — the subjective as universal.

Topography

Spatial awareness and the relationship between body, space and the other performers.
